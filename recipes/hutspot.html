Hutspot

Description:

Hutspot is a traditional Dutch dish made from mashed potatoes, carrots, and onions.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 500 g carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 500 g onions, peeled and chopped
  • 200 g smoked sausage (rookworst)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the potatoes, carrots, and onions in a large pot of salted water until tender, about 20-25 minutes.
  2. While the vegetables are cooking, heat the smoked sausage according to package instructions.
  3. Drain the vegetables and mash them together until smooth. Add butter if desired.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve the mashed vegetables with slices of smoked sausage on the side.
